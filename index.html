Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 Dec 2025

This application is designed for children and families. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to complying with all applicable privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly from users.

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data
  • No account registration or login required
  • No access to contacts, camera, microphone, or device files

Advertising

This app displays non-personalized advertisements only.

  • Ads are suitable for children
  • No interest-based or behavioral advertising
  • Ads comply with COPPA and Google Play Families policies

Advertising providers may collect limited, non-personal information such as device type, operating system, or anonymous identifiers strictly for ad serving and frequency capping.

Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

  • No personal data collection from children
  • No targeted or personalized ads
  • No social media or sharing features

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display non-personalized advertisements that are suitable for children.

Google AdMob may collect limited, non-personal information such as device type, operating system, and anonymous identifiers solely for ad serving, frequency capping, and fraud prevention, in compliance with COPPA and Google Play Families policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. Since no personal data is collected directly, the risk of misuse is minimal.
